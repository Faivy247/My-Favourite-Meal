Name of Food: Porridge Yam

A Little Info About My Favourite Meal

Porridge yam is also known as pottage yam, and it is prepared in other parts of Africa other than Nigeria. I believe other African countries have their history about the food.

Yam porridge is a delicious Nigerian yam dish cooked in a well-seasoned oil and pepper mix until soft and fluffy with some yam chunks. Some times fish and vegatables are added to make different porridge yam flavours

Ingredient Lists

S/N Ingredients Measurements
1 Yam 1 kg
2 Red palm oil Add to colour
3 Smoked fish 1
4 Crayfish 1 Table spoon ground
5 Onions 1 medium
6 Fresh green vegetables As desired
7 Pepper To taste
8 Salt To taste

Meal Preparation

Before you cook Yam Porridge

    1. Peel and cut the yam into medium sizes. Wash the yam cubes and place in a sizeable pot
    2. Wash and cut the opnions into tiny pieces. Grind or blend the chilli pepper. Set these aside
    3. If using dry fish, soak ane bones

Cooking Directions

    1. Pour enough water to cover the yam cubes and start cooking at medium to high heat. If you are using dry fish, add it now.
    2. When the yams have got a good boil, add the onions, ground crayfish, pepper, stock cubes, palm oil and the smoked fish.
    3. Cover the pot and continue cooking till the yam is done.
    4. Add salt to taste and stir very well. Cook at high heat for about 5 minutes.
    5. Add the green vegetable, stir and leave to stand for about 5 minutes then serve.
